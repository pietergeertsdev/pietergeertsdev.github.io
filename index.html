A Home Built with Passion

In 1975, a vision became reality. This remarkable home was crafted entirely by hand, using majestic oak trees harvested from the surrounding forest.

Every beam, every plank tells a story of dedication, craftsmanship, and a deep connection to the land. Built with patience and skill over countless hours, this home stands as a testament to traditional building methods and the enduring beauty of natural materials.

Nestled in a peaceful dead-end street within a charming village in Northern Belgium, this property offers a rare opportunity to own a piece of living history—a home where nature and craftsmanship unite in perfect harmony.
